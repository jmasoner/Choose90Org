## Tips for Sending

- **Personalize it:** Add the recipient's name or a personal note
- **Include the link:** Make sure to add the full URL to your Kwanzaa landing page
- **Send at the right time:** Kwanzaa runs from December 26 to January 1, so send early in that window
- **Follow up:** If someone expresses interest, offer to check in with them during the challenge
