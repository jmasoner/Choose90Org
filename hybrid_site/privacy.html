Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Choose90.org. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how we handle any information we collect.

2. Information We Collect

When you take the Pledge or Donate, we collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, and payment information (processed securely via our third-party payment providers).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Process your donations and pledges.
- Send you updates about the Choose 90 movement (only if you opt-in).
- Improve our website and community resources.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@choose90.org.
