Delete Your Account & Data

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

⚠️ Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

Your Right to Delete Your Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to do that and what data will be deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, we will permanently remove:

  • Account Information: Your email, name, screen name, and profile information
  • User Preferences: Your location, phone number, and social media handles
  • Engagement Data: Your badges, resource progress, and activity history
  • Pledge Information: Your pledge date and commitment data
  • WordPress User Account: Your entire WordPress user profile and metadata
  • Firebase Authentication: Your Firebase authentication tokens and identifiers

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and operational reasons, we may retain certain information:

  • Donation Records: Financial transaction records may be retained for tax and accounting purposes (as required by law)
  • Aggregated Analytics: Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you
  • Backup Copies: Data may exist in backups for up to 90 days before being permanently deleted

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Self-Service Deletion (Recommended)

If you're logged in:

  1. Go to your WordPress profile page
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page
  3. Click the "Delete Account" button
  4. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  5. Your account and data will be deleted immediately

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access your account or prefer to request deletion:

  1. Send an email to support@choose90.org
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Email address associated with your account
    • Request for account deletion
    • Confirmation that you understand data will be permanently deleted
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days

Firebase Authentication Data

If you signed up using Google or Facebook (Firebase Authentication), you may also need to:

  • Google Account: Visit Google Account Permissions and revoke access to Choose90
  • Facebook Account: Visit Facebook App Settings and remove Choose90

Note: We will delete your Firebase authentication data from our systems, but you should also revoke access from your Google/Facebook account settings for complete removal.

What Happens After Deletion

  1. Immediate: Your account is deactivated and you can no longer log in
  2. Within 24 hours: Your personal data is removed from our active database
  3. Within 90 days: Your data is removed from all backup systems
  4. Permanent: Your data cannot be recovered after deletion

Request a Copy of Your Data First

Before deleting your account, you may want to download a copy of your data. To request your data:

  1. Email support@choose90.org
  2. Subject line: "Data Export Request"
  3. Include your account email address
  4. We will provide your data in a machine-readable format within 30 days

Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact us:

Email: support@choose90.org
Website: choose90.org/support
Privacy Policy: choose90.org/privacy.html

✓ GDPR & CCPA Compliant: This deletion process complies with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) requirements for the right to erasure.
