## General Tips

1. **Use Kwanzaa colors:** Red, green, black, and gold in your graphics
2. **Include visuals:** Photos, graphics, or videos that represent each principle
3. **Be authentic:** Share your real experience, not just the template
4. **Engage:** Respond to comments and questions
5. **Tag appropriately:** Use relevant hashtags but don't overdo it
6. **Cross-post:** Adapt the same message for different platforms
7. **Follow up:** Post updates throughout the 7 days
