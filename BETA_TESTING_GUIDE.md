## How to Report Issues

### Option 1: Email
Send feedback to: **feedback@choose90.org**

### Option 2: Support Form
Use the contact form on: https://choose90.org/support.html

### What to Include:
- **Page/Feature:** Which page or feature had the issue?
- **Description:** What happened? What did you expect?
- **Steps to Reproduce:** How can we recreate the issue?
- **Device/Browser:** What device and browser were you using?
- **Screenshots:** If possible, include screenshots
