# WordPress Sidebar Content Guide

## Overview
The WordPress `/pledge/` page uses `get_sidebar()` which displays WordPress widgets. By default, you'll see "Recent Posts" and "Recent Comments" widgets. Here's what content to add and where to configure it.

---

## üìù Where to Add Sidebar Content

### Step 1: Access Widgets
1. Log into WordPress Admin: `https://choose90.org/wp-admin`
2. Go to **Appearance ‚Üí Widgets**
3. Find the **Sidebar** widget area (or "Main Sidebar")

### Step 2: Configure Existing Widgets

#### Recent Posts Widget
1. Find the **Recent Posts** widget in the Sidebar
2. Click to expand/edit it
3. **Settings to Configure:**
   - **Title:** "Latest from Choose90" or "Recent Blog Posts"
   - **Number of posts to show:** 3-5 (recommended)
   - **Display post date:** ‚úì Check this
   - **Display post excerpt:** ‚úì Check this (optional)

#### Recent Comments Widget
1. Find the **Recent Comments** widget in the Sidebar
2. Click to expand/edit it
3. **Settings to Configure:**
   - **Title:** "Community Voices" or "Recent Comments"
   - **Number of comments to show:** 3-5 (recommended)

**Note:** Recent Comments widget automatically displays comments on your blog posts. To populate it:

1. **Encourage Comments:**
   - Add "Share your thoughts" calls-to-action in posts
   - Respond to comments quickly
   - Ask questions in your posts

2. **Create Engaging Discussion Posts:**
   - "What does Choose90 mean to you?"
   - "Share your positive moment from today"

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Widget Not Showing?
1. Check if sidebar is enabled for your theme
2. Verify widget is added to the Sidebar area
3. Clear cache (if using caching plugin)

### No Recent Posts Showing?
1. Make sure you have published at least 1 blog post
2. Check widget settings (number of posts)
3. Verify posts are published (not draft)

### No Recent Comments Showing?
1. Make sure you have approved comments on posts
2. Check widget settings (number of comments)
3. Verify comments exist and are approved
